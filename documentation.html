Long Term Business Planning

A long-term strategy is a comprehensive plan for a business that defines goals for the vision of Promoters To create a long-term strategy, we need to set multiple smaller goals( organic/inorganic/ Top line/ Bottom Line) that helps to meet our ultimate objective.To achieve the ultimate objective, it is important to lay a roadmap. Laying a roadmap for the future is a challenging job and must be smartly handled.

A dynamic long-term financial plan must be designed with various input areas for all critical business assumptions, designing the calculation engine and providing for the outcome of the plan document. A smartly designed plan document can be an invaluable partner and guiding light for the entrepreneur as he navigates the complex business environment. Generally, plan is prepared with a 3 to 5 years view.

Budgeting & Scenario Analysis

Business environment has turned very dynamic and sometime volatile. Hence, the plan document has to provide for a smart scenario analysis as the business needs to prepare itself for all foreseen eventualities.

We assist organizations in making a simplified scenario analysis tools for the entrepreneur to make several “what if” scenarios.

Benchmarking

Immense and invaluable data is available in public domain about competitive activity, but it is scattered and requires smart assembling and analysis.

Benchmarking Financial performance against the competition can propel businesses in the right direction. It helps establish internal goals, spot market opportunities, and identify gaps in the market.
